About the Project

This project is dedicated to the prediction of breast cancer using advanced machine learning algorithms applied to ultrasound images. By employing meticulously trained models, our system provides reliable diagnostic support, aiming to aid in early detection and informed healthcare decisions.

The Creator

Sachitha S Weerasingha

As the sole developer of this project, I leveraged my expertise in machine learning and medical imaging to create a system that assists in the early diagnosis of breast cancer. My goal is to contribute to the healthcare field with technology that can save lives through early intervention.
